Our Story

We are a premium sports nutrition brand born from a simple belief: elite endurance fuel should be clean, natural, and sourced from the rich soils of Thailand.

Our Mission

Endurance sports demand everything from your body. Too often, athletes are forced to fuel their efforts with synthetic, chemical-laden syrups that cause severe gastrointestinal distress and energy crashes.

At Ener Thai, our mission is to eliminate that compromise. We build high-performance energy gels utilizing Thailand's organic, mineral-dense agricultural harvest. We create clean, biological fuel that acts in harmony with your digestive system to support peak performance.

Supporting Thai Farmers

Thailand's volcanic soils and tropical monsoons yield crops rich in mineral compounds and clean energy. We work directly with small agricultural cooperatives across the country to source our raw ingredients, establishing direct-trade contracts that bypass corporate middlemen.

By sourcing our premium ingredients directly from specialized local cooperatives—including highland Phop Phra Golden Bananas from Tak, vibrant Bang Khla Nam Dok Mai Mangoes from Chachoengsao, mineral-dense Prachuap Pattavia Pineapples from Prachuap Khiri Khan, and fresh Amphawa coconuts—we ensure fair livable wages for local farmers while securing the most pristine ingredients for endurance athletes globally.

Thailand's Finest Ingredients

Every element in an Ener Thai gel serves a functional purpose, selected for its metabolic profile:

Organic Fruit Purees: Rich in complex maltodextrins and natural fruit glucose/fructose from mango, banana, pineapple, orange, and passion fruit, providing a multi-stage carbohydrate absorption path without gut distress.

Amphawa Coconut Water: Contains a natural balance of bio-available Potassium, Sodium, and Magnesium to match electrolyte loss in sweat.

Organic Pea Protein Isolate: Provides clean, plant-based protein to halt muscle breakdown and kickstart recovery immediately after your run.

Geographical Indication (GI) & Local Pride

Premium Agricultural Origins

We carefully select only the finest agricultural zones in Thailand, where microclimates and soil conditions yield ingredients with exceptional metabolic value.

🍌 Phop Phra, Tak

Phop Phra Golden Banana
กล้วยหอมทองพบพระ อำเภอพบพระ จังหวัดตาก

Grown in the highlands of Phop Phra District in Tak Province, over 600 meters above sea level, these bananas benefit from pristine watersheds like Huay Wa Lay and the Moei River. The high altitude and cool mountain forest climate extend the growing cycle to 10–12 months (compared to 8–9 months in lowlands).

This allows the banana plant to slowly accumulate rich nutrients, resulting in an exceptionally dense, chewy texture and a sweet, aromatic flavor. A certified Geographical Indication (GI) product, it provides a highly efficient, clean source of natural carbohydrates while securing sustainable incomes for local farming communities.

🥭 Bang Khla, Chachoengsao

Bang Khla Nam Dok Mai Mango
มะม่วงน้ำดอกไม้อำเภอบางคล้า จังหวัดฉะเชิงเทรา

Bang Khla District in Chachoengsao is legendary for producing the finest Nam Dok Mai mangoes in Thailand. Blessed with mineral-rich soils and an optimal microclimate, the ripe mangoes boast a vibrant golden-yellow skin, a sweet tropical aroma, fine fiberless flesh, and a rich, velvety sweetness.

Widely celebrated as a premium culinary export to China, Taiwan, and Vietnam, we harness this sweet, easily absorbable glucose source to power rapid glycogen replenishment.

🍍 Prachuap Khiri Khan

Prachuap Pattavia Pineapple
สับปะรดพันธุ์ปัตตาเวีย จังหวัดประจวบคีรีขันธ์

Prachuap Khiri Khan is Thailand’s premier cultivation hub for the renowned Pattavia pineapple, accounting for over 80% of the country’s high-grade production. These pineapples grow large and juicy with a pale yellow flesh, balanced natural acidity, and a crisp, refreshing sweetness.

The natural bromelain and organic fruit acids provide a vibrant flavor profile that naturally settles the stomach and refreshes the palate during intense physical efforts.

Science Meets Nature

While we believe in the power of nature, we never compromise on performance science. Our formulas are engineered in sports nutrition labs and validated by professional endurance athletes.

We configure our ingredients to match human digestive pathways. The dual-source carbohydrate ratios (Maltodextrin-to-Fructose) are meticulously calibrated to avoid clogging gut receptors. We test our gels for rapid gastric emptying rates, ensuring that the natural nutrition is converted to active ATP energy as quickly as possible.

Committed to Sustainability

As runners and athletes, we are deeply connected to the natural trails and roads we run on. Protecting the environment is not a marketing catchphrase for us; it is a core business mandate.

We minimize packaging plastic usage and source post-consumer recycled cardboard for our shipping boxes. Our direct farm agreements prioritize farmers practicing organic, pesticide-free agriculture, protecting local waterways. Furthermore, a portion of every gel sale is donated to forest reforestation projects in Northern Thailand to offset our manufacturing carbon footprint.

Our Vision

"To build a global community of healthy, sustainably fueled athletes who achieve their absolute potential using natural foods that nourish the body, respect the stomach, and uplift the local agricultural communities of Thailand."
