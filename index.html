Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What makes EnerThai gels a better carb source?+
EnerThai gels combine natural fruit purees (like mango and banana) and forest honey with clean maltodextrin to provide a multi-stage carbohydrate absorption path. This ensures steady glycogen absorption without the sudden spikes and subsequent crash common in synthetic high-fructose corn syrup gels.
How does the 1:0.8 carb ratio work?+
Different carbohydrates use different transporters in the human gut. Glucose (and maltodextrin) uses the SGLT1 transporter, which saturates around 60g/hour. Fructose uses the GLUT5 transporter. By adding fructose at a 1:0.8 ratio, we open both gates, enabling athletes to absorb up to 90g+ of carbs per hour without overloading either transporter, preventing gut issues.
Do EnerThai gels require drinking water with them?+
Our STRIKE gels are formulated with coconut water concentrate and have a thin, hydrogel-like flowable texture. While they are easier to swallow without water than standard thick gels, we still recommend washing them down with a few sips of water to aid rapid gastric clearance and hydration.
